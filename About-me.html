Hello there! My name is Chihabeddine Merabet, and I was born on July 5, 2002, in Ain el kebira, Sétif, Algeria. I am 20 years old, I currently reside in El Eulma, Sétif, Algeria. As the eldest son in my family, I have two wonderful sisters and a younger brother.

Education has been a significant part of my life. I successfully completed high school with a BAC degree, achieving an impressive score of 16.06/20. Presently, I am pursuing a degree in Telecommunications at the esteemed engineering school, ENSTTIC, located in Oran.
Apart from my academic endeavors, I am an active member of the Horizon Club. Last year, I served as the Communication Responsible, and I am currently honored to hold the position of Vice-President. Being part of this club has allowed me to contribute to the university community and develop leadership skills.

In my free time, I engage in various interests and hobbies. I am a gamer, finding joy in immersing myself in virtual worlds. Additionally, I enjoy cycling, watching movies and series, as well as exploring my creative side through graphic and web design.

Organization is a key aspect of my personality. I tend to create data sheets and keep track of various aspects of my life, ensuring efficiency and structure in everything I do. I believe this trait contributes to my natural leadership abilities and allows me to navigate challenges effectively.

Looking towards the future, my aspirations are centered around building a good life, achieving financial stability, and creating a loving family. These dreams drive me to work hard and make the most of every opportunity that comes my way.
When it comes to influential figures in my life, I look no further than my parents. Their unwavering support, guidance, and sacrifices have shaped me into the person I am today.

Lastly, I have a curious belief that luck often favors me. While I cannot explain it, I appreciate the positive moments that come my way and strive to make the most of them.
